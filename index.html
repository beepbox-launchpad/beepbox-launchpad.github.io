AbyssBox:

AbyssBox is a mod of UltraBox that was created by choptop84 and was released on January 1st of 2024. Its main appeal is its unique themes, effects, and quality of life features. Its most recent version is V1.5.1.

UltraBox:

UltraBox is a mod of GoldBox that was created by Neptendo, Main, LeoV and many others, it was released on June 4th of 2023. It's main appeal is its Custom Samples, which allow you to import any sound into the mod and use them as instruments. Its current version is V2.2.15.
You can find UltraBox's Wiki Page here. Which contains extensive information about the mod.

Alternate UltraBox Versions:

UltraBox Microtonal - V2.1.0
UltraBox Offline - V2.2.15
Experimental and Testing sites are more likely prone to crashes and issues, so use with caution.

Slarmoo's Box:

Slarmoo's Box is a mod of UltraBox that was created by Slarmoo and was released on May 13th of 2024. It's main appeal is it's highly customizable envelopes, which make automation a lot more intuitive. Its current version is V1.3.8.

BeepBox:

BeepBox is a music program made by John Nesky and it was released on February 20th of 2012. It's known for being the First version of BeepBox and the originator of all other mods. Its current version is V4.2.
